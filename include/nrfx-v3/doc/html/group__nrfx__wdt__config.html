  • 1 - Black

  • 3 - Green
  • 4 - Yellow
  • 5 - Blue
  • 6 - Magenta
  • 7 - Cyan
  • 8 - White

  • 2 - 2
  • 3 - 3
  • 4 - 4 (Not applicable for nRF51)
  • 5 - 5 (Not applicable for nRF51)
